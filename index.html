Pokémon

Pokémon is a franchise of video games that is developed by Game Freak that involves the player catching and befriending creatures named pokémon which they can use to battle other pokémon in a turn based combat system. The goal of the game is to progress through the map while leveling up your pokémon in order to defeat the 8 gym leaders spread across the map which pose as the main challenge for the player and marks their progress throughout the game.

Battling System

Types

Each Pokémon can have either 1 or 2 types out of the possible 18 types. These types can determine susceptible a pokémon
can be to certain attacks, an example of this is water being strong or 'Super Effective' against types such as fire. Due to there
being 18 different pokémon types, this has caused the system to become quite intricate, however, it can be put onto a
slightly simple chart system.

class="btn" Pokémon Stats

Each Pokémon's strength can be determined by it's stats. Each Pokémon has 5 main stats which are:

  • HP - HP determines how much damage a Pokémon can receive before fainting and not being able to be used.

  • Attack - Attack or informally Physical Attack, partly determines how much damage a Pokémon deals when using a physical move.

  • Defense - Defence partly determines how much damage a Pokémon receives when it is hit with a physical move.

  • Speed - Pokémon with higher Speed at the start of any turn will generally make a move before ones with lower Speed.

  • Special Attack - Special Attack determines how much damage a Pokémon deals when using a special move.

  • Special Defense - Special Defense partly determines how much damage a Pokémon receives when it is hit with a special move.
